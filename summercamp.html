About Bal-Mukund

Bal-Mukund is a specially designed personality development program for children, conceptualized and mentored by Swami Mukundananda. Bal-Mukund is a holistic program that aims at children's physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being. Bal-Mukund has multiple centers across the globe and has been shaping the lives of thousands of children worldwide.
