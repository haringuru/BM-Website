About Bal-Mukund

Bal-Mukund is an exceptional character-building program founded by Swami Mukundananda, designed to inspire and elevate young minds. This transformative initiative offers a rich array of classes, including shloka recitation, Bhagavad Gita study, and exploration of its profound teachings. Through these classes, children are introduced to core values and virtues found in the Ramayana and Mahabharata, fostering a deep understanding of life’s principles.

Bal-Mukund’s curriculum nurtures devotion to God and appreciation for the various divine avatars, helping children develop a meaningful spiritual connection. The program is available worldwide, with sessions held at various JKYog centers as well as online, catering to children aged 5 to 15.

About Bal-Mukund

Bal-Mukund is a joyful journey where children explore their cultural roots, build character, and develop lifelong skills through engaging and playful learning. Founded by H.H. Swami Mukundananda, our program brings together ancient wisdom and modern education to nurture the mind, body, and spirit. Whether it’s through shloka recitation, yoga, or storytelling, Bal-Mukund is a loving playground that helps children grow into kind, confident, and compassionate individuals.

Our Mission

At Bal-Mukund, our mission is to empower children to build strong character, practice mindfulness, and stay connected to their cultural roots. Through value-based teachings, we aim to nurture future leaders who thrive in mind, body, and spirit, and who are ready to embrace life’s challenges with grace and resilience.

Our Vision

We envision a future where children grow into compassionate, mindful, and responsible individuals, deeply connected to their heritage and guided by strong values. Bal-Mukund strives to create a generation of children who live with purpose, kindness, and a love for learning.
